The rules

This is a turn based tactics game, inspired by games like Advance Wars, and Wargroove. It is played with currently exactly two players on a single device. Each player takes command of an army, with the goal to wipe out their opponent. To that goal they can command their units to move around the map, and attack enemy units within range. In addition to units, players may also control buildings, from which they can produce new units. Buildings can't be destroyed, but can be neutralized by the enemy, and can be captured while in a neutral state. To select a unit or building simply left click on it. If it is a unit of yours, and can still perform an action on this turn, you should see several of the surrounding tiles highlighted. Click on one of these to move to that tile. After moving a unit, or selecting a building you will be shown a context menu with the available actions. Many of those require you to select a target as well.
